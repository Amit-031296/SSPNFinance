We are semi urban and rural area focused
NBFC Microfinance company

SSPN is working dedicatedly towards offering micro to small ticket sized secured and unsecured loans to help small vendors, small business owners, it also caters to the wide range of clients opting to buy electrical devices, households, electronics ,automobile etc. up to specified ticket size. Going forward the company is planning to provide income generation loans of medium ticket size and business loans . This will be mainly cater to the large unbanked population of India.
